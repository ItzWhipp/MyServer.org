Server Rules and Guidelines

To ensure a safe and enjoyable environment for everyone, please follow these rules while playing on our server:

  • 1. Be Respectful

    Treat all players with kindness and respect. Harassment, hate speech, or any form of bullying will not be tolerated.

  • 2. No Griefing

    Do not destroy or alter other players' builds without their permission. Griefing is strictly prohibited and will result in a ban.

  • 3. No Cheating or Exploiting

    Using cheats, hacks, or exploits to gain an unfair advantage is forbidden. Play fair and enjoy the game as intended.

  • 4. Keep Chat Clean

    Avoid using offensive language or spamming in the chat. Keep conversations appropriate and fun for all players.

  • 5. Follow Staff Instructions

    Listen to and respect the instructions given by server staff. They are here to help maintain a fun and safe environment for everyone.
